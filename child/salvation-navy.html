Salvation Navy

Child

When I was in high school, I was part of the Navy Junior ROTC. Every winter we volunteered to ring bells for the Salvation Army outside of stores. We'd be in our dress blue Navy uniforms, of course. I had a kid about 6 come up to me, pointed at my uniform, and asked me, "Are you in the Salvation Navy?"
