Divine Parking

Everyday Life

An Irish man is driving through a packed parking lot, struggling to find a space.

He looks up and shouts, "Dear Lord, if You help me find a parking spot, I swear on me mother's grave that I will give up the whiskey!" Just then, a car backs out of a spot right in front of him. He looks up again and shouts, "Never mind, Lord, I found one!"
