Wal-Mart Return

Retail

One manager of a Wal-Mart said a lady came into his store wanting to return a crock pot. They started to look up the UPC code, but couldn't find it anywhere in their system. The man asked to look at the receipt, and found the lady had bought it at K-Mart. It said so, in BIG LETTERS: K-MART. The manager said he was sorry, but they didn't take K-Mart's stuff back at Wal-Mart. The lady continued to argue, then finally grabbed the crock pot off the counter, screamed at the man, "I'll NEVER shop at K-MART ever again!"
