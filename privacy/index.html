Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Introduction

Joke a Whenever ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our site, we may automatically collect:

  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, time spent on pages, and navigation patterns
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and device type
  • IP Address: Used for analytics and security purposes

Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your theme preference (light/dark mode)
  • Analyze site traffic through Google Analytics
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Improve our website and user experience
  • Analyze usage patterns and optimize content
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Maintain site security

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics
  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements

These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Disable cookies in your browser settings
  • Use browser extensions to block tracking
  • Contact us with privacy-related questions

Children's Privacy

Our website is family-friendly and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through our social media channels.
