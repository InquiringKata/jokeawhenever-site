The Sailor's Surprise

Relationship

A sailor came home from a secret two-year mission at sea only to find his wife with a newborn baby. Furious, he was determined to track down the father to extract revenge.
"Was it my friend Marvin?" he demanded.
"No!" his weeping wife replied.
"Was it my friend Jerry then?" he asked.
"NO!!!" she said even more upset.
"Well which one of my no good friends did this then?" he asked.
"Don't you think I have any friends of my own?" she snapped.
