Census Taker

Observational

I was setting on my porch, when a young man walked up with a pad and
pencil in his hand.

"What are you selling young man," I asked.
"I'm not selling anything," the joung man said. I'm the Census Taker."

"A what ?" the man asked.
"A Census Taker. We are trying to find out how many people are in the
United States."

"Well," the man answered. "You're wasting your time with me, I have no
idea.?"
