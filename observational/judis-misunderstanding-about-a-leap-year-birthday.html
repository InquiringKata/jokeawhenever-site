Judi's misunderstanding about a leap year birthday.

Observational

"A" tells me he managed Judi when she was 18. She'd complained that someone had mistaken her for 14. He replied that he'd had a "5 year old" working for him once. She tried to figure it out, "Was it one of those 'bring your kid to work' things?" Finally "A" told her that the "5 year old" had 2/29 as a birthday and had only celebrated 5 birthdays. Judi swore she'd never heard of such a thing and actually asked, "So . . . did she like . . . not *age*, then?"
