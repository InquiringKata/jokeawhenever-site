Ocean-Lover's Adventures

Observational

I'm running around freely, like an undaunted child, while still taking care of AllWorld business. I balance life with long walks by the ocean's side. Yes, I said "ocean." Yippie!!

Boy, God, you sure do good work with all this scenery, too! That "Haystack Rock" scene is really something! :-)

And think nothing of my mentioning it, Buddy... Always happy to admire your work.

Well, for those of you who've followed my "Vacation Adventures," you know that you know that I simply LOVE the ocean. I just can't get enough, in fact, even with the storms and dampness. (By the way, I also think raging storms are the bomb! I can't wait to experience the first gale of the season.)

I finally gave myself what I wanted, and I've never felt better. See, I've gone "off my rocker" and moved right in! Yep, my U-Haul is waiting outside the motel now, patiently foreseeing being unloaded into my new home in the morning. *silly grin*

So, what's this mean to you? Simple! Today's issue is a special treat. You can call it Adventures of the Move, or the acronym W. I. D. O. M. A. V. ? may do. There's humor and real-life insights within, most too obvious to overlook by anyone even slightly awake.

Some would say so, while others look forward to something different from life, and change in AllWorld's Joke of the Day! is no different.
