Snowman Building

Children

The father watched through the window as his young daughter made a snowman with a little friend.
Entertained by the sight, he went closer and heard the little boy say:
"I've got an idea. To finish it off, I'll go to the kitchen and find a carrot."
And his daughter replied, "Make it two. The second can be his nose."
