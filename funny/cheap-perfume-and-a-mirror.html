Cheap Perfume and a Mirror

Funny

After being away on business, Clarence McDougal thought it would be nice to bring his wife a little gift. "How about some perfume?" he asked the cosmetics clerk. She showed him a bottle costing $50.00. "That is a bit much," said Clarence. So the clerk returned with a smaller bottle for $30.00. "That's still quite a bit," Clarence groused. Growing annoyed, the cosmetics clerk brought out a tiny $15.00 bottle. "What I mean," said Clarence, "is I would like to see something really cheap." So the clerk handed him a mirror.
